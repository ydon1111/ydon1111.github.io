Why D Lab

Digital healthcare leverages technology such as the Internet of Things (IoT) and artificial intelligence (AI) to collect, analyze, and manage medical data. This Data-driven approach enhances patient care, Diagnoses & Decision support and treatment by enabling real-time monitoring, predictive analytics, and personalized medicine. It also promotes remote healthcare Delivery through telemedicine and mobile health apps, improving accessibility and healthcare efficiency while transforming how healthcare is delivered and experienced.
