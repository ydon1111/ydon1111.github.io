MISSION & VISION

Our mission is to harness the collective power of knowledge and cutting-edge technology across diverse domains to deliver innovative solutions that promote human well-being, with a particular emphasis on advancing healthcare practices that contribute to a healthier and happier existence. In parallel, we are dedicated to nurturing a cadre of imaginative and resilient professionals capable of perpetuating this quest.

HISTORY

D.Lab opened in March 2022 at Asan Medical Center, University of Ulsan College of Medicine. DLab's predecessor was Healthcare Solution Lab., which was originally established at the College of Engineering Science at Chonnam National University. It was later renewed as D.Lab when Professor Hangsik Shin, the Principal Investigator (PI), relocated to Asan Medical Center, University of Ulsan College of Medicine

COLLABORATION

D. Lab thrives on collaborative research, actively engaging with esteemed academics and industry leaders across engineering and medicine. We continually seek to expand our horizons by collaborating with experts from diverse domains. If you're a passionate researcher or a forward-thinking company interested in joining hands with D. Lab, we warmly invite you to reach out to us through the contact details provided on our website. Let's explore new frontiers together.

PARTICIPATION

D. Lab offers unique opportunities for students enrolled in the Ulsan University Graduate School of Medical Science and AMIST (Asan Medical Institute of Convergence Science and Technology, http://amist.ac.kr/), as well as individuals interested in collaborating with Asan Medical Center, University of Ulsan College of Medicine.

We are currently seeking dedicated professionals to join our team in various roles, including research professors, postdoctoral researchers, and researchers. If you have a passion for cutting-edge research and innovation, we invite you to consider a rewarding career with D. Lab.
