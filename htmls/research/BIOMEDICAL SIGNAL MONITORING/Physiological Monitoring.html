Biomedical Signal Monitoring

Physiological Monitoring

Our pioneering research focuses on the comprehensive realm of IoT-based bio-signal monitoring, encompassing the development of cutting-edge sensors, robust systems, innovative applications, and advanced algorithms. With a steadfast commitment to advancing healthcare, our mission revolves around creating a user-friendly and accessible ecosystem, enabling individuals to seamlessly monitor their well-being in diverse settings, from hospital environments to the rhythms of everyday life. Our extensive portfolio of breakthrough technologies spans the monitoring of vital human parameters, including blood pressure, electrocardiogram (ECG), respiration, gait analysis, and sleep patterns. Through this holistic approach, we are dedicated to transforming the landscape of personal health management, contributing to healthier, more informed lives for individuals around the world.

Wearable Blood Pressure Monitoring

Our ongoing research endeavors to provide a promising method for estimating continuous blood pressure waveforms without the need for uncomfortable cuff pressurization. By harnessing the potential of multimodal measurement techniques, integrating multi-wavelength optical sensors and multi-channel pressure sensors, we're on the brink of transforming blood pressure monitoring into a more comfortable and accessible experience.


*The protocol of this study was approved (IRB no. 1-2019-0080) by the Institutional Review Board (IRB) of Yonsei University Severance Hospital (Seoul, South Korea)

Non-Constrained Sleep Monitoring

Sleep, a fundamental aspect occupying nearly one-third of our lives, holds a treasure trove of insights into our physical and mental well-being. Our ongoing research is dedicated to the development of cutting-edge systems and analysis technologies designed to unveil the mysteries of sleep without any physical constraints. While the technology is still undergoing rigorous testing and refinement, we are committed to revolutionizing the way we understand and monitor our vital physiological activity during this essential restorative process.

Respitation Monitoring

Amidst the unprecedented global challenges brought forth by the COVID-19 pandemic, the demand for respiratory disease monitoring has reached unprecedented heights. In response to this urgent need, we've embarked on a groundbreaking endeavor to monitor breathing patterns through the detection of abdominal or chest movement, pressure or temperature changes generated by inhalation and exhalation. Our approach involves seamlessly integrating this technology into masks, harnessing the potential to respiratory health monitoring in this critical environment.


*The protocol of this study was approved (IRB no. 1-2019-0080) by the Institutional Review Board (IRB) of Yonsei University Severance Hospital (Seoul, South Korea)

Belt-type ECG monitoring

In our ongoing study, we proudly introduce a pioneering ECG measurement system that seamlessly integrates into daily life by wearing it around the waist, much like a belt. While wearable ECG technology has gained traction, our unique approach stands out for its remarkable ability to monitor vital biosignals without disrupting everyday routines. Though the technology is currently in its testing phase, it holds the promise of revolutionizing health monitoring while preserving the natural cadence of daily behaviors.


*The protocol of this study was approved by the Institutional Review Board (IRB) of of Chonnam National University (Gwangju, South Korea) biomedical research ethics committee (IRB no. 1040198-200609-HR-061 -02).
