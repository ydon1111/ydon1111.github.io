Biomedical Signal Monitoring

Sleep Monitoring

Sleep constitutes a significant portion of our lives, accounting for roughly one-third of our time. Sleep-related issues can potentially lead to problems in our daily lives, underscoring the importance of maintaining good sleep health for overall well-being. Furthermore, sleep serves as a window during which our bodies can potentially reveal if something might be amiss. Thus, sleep analysis becomes a means to understand our physical and mental health status in everyday life. In this research field, we investigate how our movements unfold during sleep and decipher the signals our bodies emit. Through this exploration, we gain insights into the potential for sleep-related problems or other health issues to arise.

Unconstrained Sleep Monitoring

This burgeoning field of research focuses on the development of sensors, systems, and platforms capable of measuring behaviors and biological signals occurring during sleep, all without the need for physical restraint or conscious awareness on the part of the human body.

Actigraphy on Sleep

This cutting-edge innovation utilizes a wristband-type actigraph to record and meticulously analyze movements during sleep, providing a compreensive and real-time evaluation of sleep quality.
