High-Risk Patient Monitoring

The purpose of this project is to establish a platform for real-time monitoring of physiological data of high-risk patients among those who are not continuously monitored in the hospital, including general inpatients, emergency patients, and patients waiting for treatment. The goal is to develop technology for collecting patient physiological information (heart rate, activity level) using personal wearable devices and to aim for the development of algorithms for detecting abnormal and anomalous states based on the collected patient physiological data.

High-risk patient monitoring platform based on Responsive web application

The figure shows that is to build a platform capable of collecting location information, heart rate, and activity level data for various high-risk groups within the hospital. Heart rate and activity levels are collected from wearable watches and are integrated with the platform server via WiFi communication. Location information is collected through BLE (Bluetooth Low Energy) communication between the wearable watch and the location information scanner.
