Digital Medicine

Medical Device Dev. Tool

The purpose of this project is to explore the sleep analysis capabilities of Home Sleep Apnea Testing (HSAT) as an alternative to the traditionally used Polysomnography (PSG). Our primary focus is to scrutinize approval trends for sleep analysis devices, elucidate their evaluation criteria, and establish a framework for the universal and objective assessment of devices that are currently self-verified by manufacturers. Furthermore, by developing a simulator tailored to generate events associated with sleep disorders and stages, we aim to enhance clinical efficiency and reliability. Ultimately, our research aims to set a robust and objective foundation for the evaluation of sleep analysis technology.
