Photoplethysmogram Analysis

PPG (Photoplethysmography) is a technology that measures heart rate and blood flow information by utilizing reflected light. Placed on the skin, sensors analyze changes in the absorption and reflection of light, allowing for the measurement of parameters such as heart rate, heart rate variability, and blood flow status. This technology finds primary applications in the field of healthcare, with significant clinical potential for monitoring cardiovascular health, diagnosing heart conditions, and tracking sleep patterns. Furthermore, it can be harnessed through wearable devices for routine health monitoring, offering personalized healthcare services. In the future, PPG is expected to play a pivotal role in healthcare management and early disease detection.
